What To Work On

  • - Devlope more graphics for users to generate information from such as a graph or table for a visual overview of expenses.
  • - Spend more time practicing the art of putting your brand into marketing materials. This will amplify the way potential users are attracted to your product.
  • - Testing during COVID 19 has created difficulty in getting users properly prepared and to provide adquate feedback during the testing process. Develop better ways to connect to your testers remotely, preparing and guiding them in more astute ways to ensure better results.

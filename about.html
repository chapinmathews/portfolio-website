About

My Story

Where are you based? I am currently based in Richmond Virginia. How do your spend your free time? I find inspiration through traveling and exploring new locations and cultures, whether domestic or international. I am an avid film photographer which is a skill that accompanies every travel opportunity. When I am not traveling and working on photography, I’m usually playing guitar or tuning into a good film. What’s a fun fact about you? In 2014 I received my Yoga Teacher Training Certificate from Shoshoni Yoga Ashram in Rollinsville, CO and in 2017 received a meditation certification with my teacher Yashoda Devi Ma. Both of these modalities are daily disciplines that greatly increase my quality of life. What’s your professional experience? In 2009 I received a Bachelor of Fine Arts (BFA) degree in Material Studies from Virginia Commonwealth University (VCU) in Richmond, VA. Upon graduating, I spent the next 10 years traveling and living across the United States as a creative producer. From 2010 to 2015, I was predominately a studio painter and began reteaching myself graphic design as requests for logos, album covers, and event posters came in. In 2016, I reemerged creatively as a photographer and produced a photojournalism project titled “Diverse Dwellings”, covering stories on alternative living along the west coast. I went on to assist the obscure fashion-photographer Bil Brown in Los Angeles in 2017. In 2018 I was the executive producer for a short film titled “ORIGIN” where I managed a cast and crew of 23 people and a $70k production budget. These experiences culminated a path towards becoming a designer. Why do you think UI Design is valuable? UI Design enables designers to create interactive products for digital devices that provide solutions for the product’s user base. This design evolution into the digital age has been increadibly important to how we experience life and solve problems today. UI Design is a key fundamental in creating balance between the visual esthetic of a product and the functionality and purpose it provides.
